About Me

I am an MBA candidate at the University of Oklahoma, a U.S. Army veteran, and a professional with experience in facilities capital planning, consulting, and entrepreneurship. I am passionate about bringing structured problem-solving and data-driven decision-making to complex business challenges.

My long-term goal is to merge my leadership background with technology consulting expertise, focusing on operational backbones, digital platforms, and community-driven projects. This website serves as my digital resume and a collection of the projects, books, and ideas that shape my work.
